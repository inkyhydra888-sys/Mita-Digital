FAQ

Questions people actually ask.

How does payment work?

Pay via EFT or cash. If you'd prefer to swap goods or services, just let me know what you offer and we'll agree on a fair exchange before I start work.

Do I need to come in for a meeting?

Never. Everything happens over WhatsApp and email — we work at your pace, in your own time. No awkward Zoom calls required.

How long does a website take to build?

A Starter site typically takes 3–5 working days. A Premium site with lots of integrations takes about 7–10 days. Timelines can vary depending on how quickly you send content and feedback.

What do I need to provide?

Your logo (if you have one), any photos you want used, and a description of your business and services. You'll also need to purchase a domain name — I'll tell you exactly what to get and where. No logo? Add a branding package and I'll create one for you.

What do I get when the site is done?

Everything. All logins, your domain linked and live, a brand new email address (Gmail, Proton Mail, or a business email on request), plus full documentation for your site and/or templates so you always know how to manage things yourself.

Will my website work on phones?

Always. Every site I build is fully mobile-responsive — looks and works great on phones, tablets, and desktop.

Can you help if I'm outside South Africa?

Absolutely. I work remotely with clients worldwide. Swaps from international clients are welcome as long as goods can be shipped.
